Common Microwave Oven Faults We Fix

Below is a list of common microwave oven issues we address during Microwave Oven Repair in Runda, each with a brief description of the problem and its impact.

Microwave Not Heating

The microwave runs but fails to heat food, often due to a faulty magnetron or diode. This is one of the most common issues requiring professional repair.

Sparking Inside Microwave

Sparks inside the microwave can indicate a damaged waveguide cover or exposed metal, posing a safety hazard that needs immediate attention.

Turntable Not Rotating

A non-rotating turntable prevents even heating, often caused by a faulty motor or damaged roller guide.

Microwave Not Starting

If the microwave doesn’t turn on, the issue could stem from a blown fuse, faulty door switch, or control board failure.

Unusual Noises

Loud humming or buzzing noises may indicate issues with the magnetron, fan, or other internal components.

Types of Microwave Ovens

The Best Microwave Oven Repair in Nairobi caters to various microwave types, each with unique features and repair requirements. Below is an overview of the most common microwave types we service, presented in a grid format for clarity.

Countertop Microwaves

Compact and portable, these microwaves are ideal for small kitchens. They are easy to access for repairs but may require specific parts for different brands.

Over-the-Range (OTR) Microwaves

Installed above stovetops, OTR microwaves save counter space and often include ventilation systems. Repairs may involve additional costs due to installation complexity.

Built-In Microwaves

Integrated into kitchen cabinetry, these microwaves offer a sleek look but require precise repairs to maintain aesthetic consistency.

Convection Microwaves

Combining microwave and convection cooking, these units are versatile but complex, often requiring specialized repairs for components like fans and heating elements.
